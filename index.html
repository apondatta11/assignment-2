Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The kindergarten curriculum focuses on foundational skills like early literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional development. Through play-based learning, children explore basic reading, writing, and counting while developing creativity, communication, and motor skills. Activities include storytelling, singing, hands-on projects, and interactive play to foster curiosity, independence

Elementary School

Elementary education builds core academic skills in language, mathematics, science, and social studies. It encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving through structured lessons and hands-on activities. Subjects like arts, physical education, and technology are integrated to support holistic development. The curriculum nurtures curiosity .

Read More

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school bridges elementary and high school, introducing complex subjects like algebra, literature analysis, and scientific inquiry. It emphasizes independent thinking, teamwork, and self-discipline. Electives, extracurriculars, and project-based learning enhance creativity and leadership. Social and emotional growth is nurtured alongside academics, preparing students for future academic and personal challenges .
